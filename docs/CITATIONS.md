# Citations And Parameter Traceability

HydroOnc v1.0.0 keeps parameter provenance in
`packages/ml_pipeline/src/hydroonc_pipeline/citations.py`.

Representative references:

- CODATA 2018 recommended values for physical constants.
- NIST PSTAR tables for proton stopping power in water.
- ICRU Report 78 for clinical proton RBE = 1.1.
- CEST MRI tumor acidosis literature for tumor extracellular pH ranges.
- Electrochemical transport references for proton diffusion in water.
- Molecular hydrogen kinetics literature for H2 + hydroxyl radical and
  peroxynitrite rate constants.
- Radiation chemistry references for GSH hydroxyl radical scavenging.
- Akagi 2019/2020 and Chen 2020 small H2 oncology immune studies.
- TP53 structural oncology literature for p53 hotspot mutations.
- KRAS G12C co-crystal literature for sotorasib/adagrasib His95 interactions.
- EGFR resistance structural literature for T790M and C797S.
- FAST-01 JAMA Oncology 2023 for FLASH feasibility.
